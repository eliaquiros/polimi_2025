DESCRIPTION OF THE LESSON

  • Automatic extraction of stream networks from DEMs.
  • Common flow extraction algorithms:
    •  D8
    •  D∞
    •  KRA

  • Statistical analysis

    • Statistical graphics

                 

Examples of circular data distributions (azimuths) plotted in (from left to right) raw plot, circular histogram and circular-linear plot.
